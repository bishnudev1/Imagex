1. Download your preffered image (Must be png format).
2. Then named it to "Original".
3. Don't delete the Sketch image file, it may crash the app.
4. Save it to App directory.
5. Open the App by double clicking it.
6. Now you can see your Original image in the left section.
7. Simply click on "Convert" button.
8. Close the App and check the App directory.Your Sketch image will be stored there.
9. Enjoy & Happy Coding !
